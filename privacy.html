Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Lumiqo is designed to be a local-first application where your documents remain your own.

Effective Date: June 21, 2026
Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Lumiqo Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Lumiqo PDF Reader mobile application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our practices regarding the handling of your information.

Strict Privacy Commitment: Lumiqo is a local utility. We do NOT collect, upload, or share the content of your PDF files. All processing (merging, reading, and renaming) occurs strictly on your device.

1. Information We Handle

Lumiqo is designed to function without requiring personal user accounts.

A. User-Generated Content (PDFs)

The app processes PDF documents. These files never leave your device. We do not have access to your documents.

B. Locally Stored Data

To improve your experience, the following data is stored only on your device (using Jetpack DataStore):

  • Reading Goals & Progress (Minutes read per day)
  • App Preferences (Theme selection, Language settings)
  • Library Metadata (Recent files and Favorites)

2. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality as a file manager and editor, Lumiqo requires:

  • All Files Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Essential for scanning your device to build your PDF library and allowing the app to merge/save documents to public folders.
  • Internet & Notifications: Required to deliver important app updates, feature announcements, and reading reminders via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted industry providers solely for app maintenance and communication:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Sends push notifications. FCM may collect an anonymous "Instance ID" to deliver messages.
  • Google Play Services: Used for secure app distribution and crash reporting.

4. Monetization & Payments

Lumiqo Premium features (future update) are processed via Razorpay. We do not store or see your credit card or financial identifiers on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Lumiqo does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our app is a general productivity tool.
