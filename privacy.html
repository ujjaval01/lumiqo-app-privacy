Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Lumiqo Labs handles your data with transparency and security.

Effective Date: June 21, 2026
Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Lumiqo Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Lumiqo PDF Reader mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Strict Privacy Commitment: Lumiqo does not collect, upload, or share the content of your PDF files. All document processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data from you unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., when sending feedback). Lumiqo is designed to be a privacy-first application.

A. Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain identifiers for specific features:

  • Email address (only if you contact support)
  • Phone number (only for payment processing via Razorpay)
  • Usage Data (anonymized crash reports via Google)

2. Permissions

To provide its functionality, Lumiqo requires the following permissions on your Android device:

  • Storage/Media Access: Required to find, open, and display PDF files saved on your device.
  • Internet Access: Required for premium subscription processing and checking for app updates.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to facilitate premium features and app stability:

  • Razorpay: Processes premium subscriptions securely. They are PCI-DSS compliant. View Policy
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and crash monitoring.

4. Payment Security

When you purchase Lumiqo Premium, your payment details (Credit Card, UPI, etc.) are processed directly by Razorpay. We do not store or collect your financial data on our servers.

5. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
